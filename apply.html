Aarushi Gupta Memorial Scholarship Program

Apply

The Fund exists to give, not to ask. If higher education is within your reach but the cost is not, this is for you.

What the Fund gives

Four kinds of support

Tuition assistance

Help with the fees that stand between a student and their course.

Stipend

Support for the everyday cost of studying, so learning is not a luxury.

Mentorship

Guidance from people who have walked the path the student is on.

Competition support

Assistance for domestic and international academic competitions.

Who it is for

Deserving, meritorious students

  • Academic excellence in their field of study.
  • Leadership, shown in how they carry themselves and others.
  • Competitive superiority, the drive to reach further.
  • Genuine financial need, so that cost is the only thing standing in the way.

How to apply

Through your institution

Scholarships are nominated through your school, college, or university. The path is short.

  1. 1 Raise it with your institution’s head. Tell the head of your institution you would like to be considered for the Aarushi Gupta Memorial Scholarship.
  2. 2 Your institution shortlists. The institution reviews candidates against the criteria and selects those it will put forward.
  3. 3 It forwards candidates to the Fund. The Fund considers the shortlist and follows up directly. You are welcome to write to us at any point.

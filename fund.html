We give. We do not ask.

Why, in her name

In the family’s words

Aarushi’s life was marked by compassion, and a deep appreciation for learning and personal growth. While her time with us was far too short, her values continue to inspire those who knew and loved her.

As a family, we wish to create a meaningful and lasting tribute that would carry her legacy forward by supporting students seeking financial help to fulfil their educational journeys.

What it offers

Higher education, without the constraint of cost

The Fund supports deserving and meritorious students in pursuing higher education without financial constraint. Support takes the form of tuition fee assistance, stipends, mentorship, and help to take part in domestic and international academic competitions.

The eligibility, the four forms of support, and the deadlines are set out on the application page.

How it reaches students

Through their institutions

A student raises it with their institution’s head; the institution shortlists candidates and forwards them to the Fund. Students and institutions are both welcome to write to contact@aarushiguptamemorial.org.
